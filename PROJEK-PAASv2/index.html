Mood Disorder

A type of mental disorder that causes the sufferer to experience happiness or sadness beyond normal limits (mood swings).

Depression

A type of mental disorder that makes it difficult for the sufferer to distinguish between reality and hallucinations, caused by physical exhaustion or a disturbed mental state.

learn more

Impulse Control Disorder

A type of mental disorder that makes it difficult for the sufferer to restrain themselves from acting aggressively.

learn more

Eating Disorder

A type of mental disorder that makes it difficult for the sufferer to control the quantity of food they consume; this disorder can develop into Bulimia Nervosa.

learn more

OCD (Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder)

A type of mental disorder that causes the sufferer to repeatedly engage in certain activities. If not performed, the sufferer will experience excessive anxiety.

learn more

Personality Disorder

A type of mental disorder that causes the sufferer to have abnormal behavior and thought patterns, making it difficult for them to understand situations and other people.

learn more

Psychosomatic Disorder

A type of mental disorder that causes the sufferer to experience physical problems due to their thoughts, such as fatigue, muscle pain, and sweaty palms.

learn more

Factitious Disorder

A type of mental disorder that causes the sufferer to pretend to have a physical or psychological illness. This can lead to the sufferer deliberately faking symptoms or even harming themselves to gain attention from others.

learn more

PTSD (Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder)

A type of mental disorder that causes the sufferer to feel anxious and overreact, triggered by seeing an event similar to their past trauma.
